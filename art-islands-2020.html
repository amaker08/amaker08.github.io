The Moving Box

A box will be disfatched from the artist’s atelier in Korea to Takada-san in Japan. The box contains five “pebbles” made out of crayon pieces and layered with drawing paper. As the box moves, the pebbles would roll and leave traces on the paper – as they record their journey. This drawing is completed with the distance from the sender to the recipient and the time it takes to travel between two places.
